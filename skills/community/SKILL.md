# Community Building Skill

## Overview
Build, grow, and nurture developer communities that drive product adoption and create lasting value for members.

## Core Topics

### Community Strategy
- Defining community goals and success metrics
- Community platforms (Discord, Slack, Forums)
- Community governance and moderation
- Ambassador and champion programs

### Engagement Tactics
- Onboarding new community members
- Recognition and reward systems
- Regular programming (AMAs, office hours, hackathons)
- User-generated content encouragement

### Community Health
- Measuring community engagement
- Identifying and addressing toxic behavior
- Conflict resolution
- Preventing burnout (yours and members')

### Scaling Community
- From 100 to 1000 to 10000 members
- Volunteer and moderator programs
- Automation and tooling
- Regional community chapters

## Learning Objectives
- Design effective community strategies
- Implement engagement programs
- Measure and improve community health
- Scale community operations sustainably
