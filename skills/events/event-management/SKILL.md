# Developer Event Management

Plan and execute **successful developer events** that create lasting impact.

## Event Types

| Type | Size | Duration | Effort |
|------|------|----------|--------|
| Meetup | 20-100 | 2-3 hours | Low |
| Workshop | 10-50 | Half/full day | Medium |
| Hackathon | 50-500 | 24-48 hours | High |
| Conference | 200-2000 | 1-3 days | Very High |
| Webinar | 50-500 | 1 hour | Low |

## Event Planning Timeline

### 3-6 Months Before
- Define goals and success metrics
- Set budget
- Book venue (if in-person)
- Recruit speakers
- Create event branding

### 2-3 Months Before
- Open registration
- Launch promotion
- Finalize agenda
- Confirm sponsors
- Plan logistics

### 1 Month Before
- Speaker prep calls
- Finalize catering/A/V
- Send attendee reminders
- Prepare materials
- Test technology

### Day Before
- Venue walkthrough
- Test all equipment
- Brief volunteers
- Prepare swag bags
- Final speaker check

## Promotion Strategy

```
Email → Social → Partners → Community → Press
  ↓        ↓         ↓           ↓         ↓
List     Posts   Co-promo    Members    PR
Sequence Campaign  Shares   Announce   Outreach
```

## Post-Event

| Activity | Timing |
|----------|--------|
| Thank you emails | Same day |
| Recording publish | 1 week |
| Feedback survey | 1-3 days |
| Photo gallery | 1 week |
| Blog recap | 2 weeks |
| ROI report | 2-4 weeks |

## Success Metrics

- Registration to attendance rate
- Net Promoter Score
- Content engagement
- Lead generation
- Community growth
