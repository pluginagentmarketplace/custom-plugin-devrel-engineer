## Time Expectations

### Learning Timelines

**Beginner to Intermediate**: 6-12 months
- Daily 1-2 hours of focused learning
- Regular hands-on practice
- Building small projects

**Intermediate to Advanced**: 12-24 months
- Daily 2-3 hours of practice
- Large projects
- Deep specialization

**Proficiency**: 2-3 years
- Full-time dedicated work
- Real production experience
- Problem-solving expertise

**Expert Level**: 5+ years
- Industry experience
- Teaching capability
- Innovation ability
