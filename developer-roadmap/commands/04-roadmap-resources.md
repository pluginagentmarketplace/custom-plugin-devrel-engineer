## Choosing Your Resources

### Combination Strategy
1. **Pick 1 structured course** - Build foundation
2. **Get 1 reference book** - Deep learning
3. **Use official documentation** - Specifics
4. **Join a community** - Support & motivation
5. **Practice regularly** - Hands-on skills

### Time Investment

**Per Day:**
- 1 hour: Course/book
- 30 min: Practice problems
- 20 min: Documentation reading
- 10 min: Community engagement

**Total: ~2 hours/day for faster progress**
